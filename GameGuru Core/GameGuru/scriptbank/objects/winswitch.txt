Winswitch behavior

This object will be treated as a switch object to end the level.

Behavior options:
Edit the PROMPT TEXT eg: "to End Level"
Play the audio <Sound0> when the object is switched ON by the player.
Select ToLevelMode for 'Use Storyboard Logic" or 'Go to Specific Level' to control
whether the next level in the Storyboard, or another level is loaded after the 
switch is turned on.
